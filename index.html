Monkeys

Monkeys are a furry primate which come in many different sizes between small to medium-sized. They typically have a long tail, and hands for feet. There are about 260 known living species of monkey which can be sepreated into two groups called New world Monkeys and Old World Monkeys.. Most kinds of Monkeys live in trees in tropical countries, and are generally considered to be intelligent.

Feeding Habits

Monkeys have unique feeding habits. depending on the species, monkeys will consume different sources of food. Though they differ in diet, monkeys have similar behaviours which are derived from their physical needs, their intelligence and their habitat.

Habitat

Generally, monkeys live in forested areas with many trees although some species are known to live in the savannahs and mountain regiosn. Some monkeys live in trees and on land while other monkeys live exclusively in trees.
